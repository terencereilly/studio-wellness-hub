What is Mental Wellbeing?

Your mental health shifts day to day. You can take small steps to stay grounded and supported.

Understanding Stress

Stress is a normal reaction. Learning to notice early signs helps you manage it more easily.

Small Steps That Help

Simple habits like taking breaks or talking to someone you trust can make a meaningful difference.

Spotting the Signs of Stress

Everyone experiences stress differently, common signs include:

• Difficulty concentrating • Feeling overwhelmed • Changes in sleep patterns • Tension or restlessness
